    Cubix named a Clutch 1000 company!

    “Asian markets hold tremendous potential in the tech world, and we’ve had the pleasure of working on some exciting projects there—thanks to Clutch, we’ve enjoyed incredible exposure to serious clients.”

    — Salman Lakhani, CEO, Cubix

    As an expert at Forbes writes, choosing the right software development partner can be a challenge. When there’s a whole sea of software developers out there, it’s difficult to know which agencies and providers can effectively deliver.

    Here at Cubix, we’re transforming the digital landscape by delivering unique software solutions. But what are some of our credentials?

    We’re glad you asked! When it comes to B2B credibility, Clutch— a top ratings and reviews site—can be a great resource. And, we’re proud to announce that we’ve been recognized as a Top 1000 company by Clutch. This is a huge deal because Clutch features real reviews left by verified clients.

    On the platform, we boast a near-perfect rating of 4.9. You can also see the rave reviews left by many of our happy customers:

    “Cubix’s ability to rapidly prototype projects based on constant input from the client is very impressive. The quality of the final product and its adherence to the client’s specifications are excellent.” — Timothy Panania, Founder, Coastal Games, Inc.

    They designed an exact replica of the app I visualized inside of my head without any guidance from visual aids. Beyond their talent, they’re an honest and pleasant company to work with. — Mario Salvo, Owner, Birdegreetings.

    Since we’re fueled by customer satisfaction, we’re delighted to be recognized by such a client-driven platform. Clutch has two other B2B resources: The Manifest, a business blog that’ll guide your purchasing decisions, and Visual Objects, which shows portfolio items of the best service providers.

    This award only motivates us to keep delivering top-notch digital solutions. So, what are you waiting for? Let’s start a project together today.
